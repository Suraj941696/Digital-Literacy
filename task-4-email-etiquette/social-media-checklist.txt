Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist (College Students)


Do’s
1. Keep your profile professional and clean.
2. Use privacy settings for your information.
3. Be cautious before posting anything online.
4. Be sure of the information before sharing it with others.
5. Use a strong password and activate Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).


Don’ts
1. Don’t share OTP, UPI PIN, passwords, and other information.
2. Don’t share offensive and inappropriate content.
3. Don’t click unknown and suspicious links.
4. Don’t accept random friend requests from strangers.
5. Don’t practice online bullying, trolling, and hate speeches.
