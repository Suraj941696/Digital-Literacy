* Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students)




General Online Safety Tips
1. Use strong passwords for all accounts.
2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for Gmail, Instagram, LinkedIn, and banking apps.
3. Don’t click any suspicious links received through SMS, WhatsApp, Email, or unknown websites.
4. Always check the website URL before logging in.
5. Don’t share OTP, passwords, and other information with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
6. Don’t download apps from unknown sources.
7. Don’t use public Wi-Fi for banking, UPI transactions, and other sensitive information.
8. Keep your phone, antivirus, and apps updated.


Financial / UPI Safety Tips (Mandatory)
9. Don’t enter UPI PIN for receiving funds.
10. Don’t accept unknown “Collect Requests” for Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm.
11. Don’t scan unknown QR codes shared by strangers.
12. Immediately block your UPI/bank account if fraud is suspected.


Reporting Cybercrime (India)
If you are a victim of cybercrime, report it immediately.
- National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in  
- Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
